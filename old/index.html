Dream 4TK

Dream4TK

Four riders. 1,500-mike bike ride from New Brunswick, NJ to Disney World in Orlando, Florida. $1,000,000 for the kids. Every great success begins with a dream!


"Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed people can change the world. Indeed, it is the only thing that ever has." - Margaret Mead, American cultural anthropologist


On July 28, 2013, our four riders will leave from New Brunswick, NJ and embark on a 1,500-mile journey to Orlando, Florida. Dream4TK aims to promote awareness, foster community and raise $1,000,000 for Embrace Kids Foundation.

Embrace Kids Foundation is a charity that helps provide for the non-medical needs of families of children with cancer and blood disorders. The charity offers a number of services including emergency financial support, trauma counselors, tutors and child-life specialists. The foundation is committed to enhancing the quality of life of the children and to relieving families of emotional and financial pressures.
