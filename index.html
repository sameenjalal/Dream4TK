Dream4TK

What? - 1,500 mile bike ride

Where? - New Brunswick, NJ to the Land of Dreams, Disney World in Orlando, FL

When? - Sunday, July 28th to Sunday, August 25th

Why? - To raise $1,000,000 to further the pursuit of dreams

Who? - The 5 riders and the dream team

Embrace Kids Foundation exists to help families whose children have cancer or blood disorders. The foundation is committed to enhancing the quality of life of the children and to relieving families of emotional and financial pressures.

Dream Week was a week to announce to the Rutgers University community that a group of dreamers plans to bike 1,500 miles and raise $1,000,000 for the kids.

The Dream Team is looking to further expand our team and have more individuals work with us to achieve our goals! We have multiple teams that are working hard every day to make this dream come true. We can't do this without your help!
